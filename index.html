About Me.

My heritage is very important to me both my parents are Native American. My father is from The Sisseton Wahpeton Oyate of the Lake Traverse Reservation, formerly Sisseton-Wahpeton Sioux Tribe/Dakota Nation. My mother was born in Southastern Alaska and is 1/2 Scandinavian and 1/2 Tlingit. Most of my mothers family are commercial fisherman and we was really fortuante that they would send down fresh seafood for all the family members. During my summers growing up my parents, bother and I would head back to my dads reservation to visit his brothers and sister for 2 weeks. In those 2 weeks there was the huge Pow Wow everyone would go to, the food was RIDICULOUSLY AMAZING (both at my families houses and at the Pow Wow!)

Top 3 Foods sold at the Pow Wow

  1. Fry Bread.
  2. Indian Taco's
  3. Special Corn and Bison soup.
